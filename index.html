Why pay for PDF Formatting?
The PDF format is a mess of conflicting specifications. Automated software conversion often deliver garbled output. Manual work by trained formatters is required for a nice, editable output.

Yes, we aim to provide an editable file. In some rare case, it might not be possible or it might take longer. Send us your file & we'll tell you what can be done, when & for how much.
